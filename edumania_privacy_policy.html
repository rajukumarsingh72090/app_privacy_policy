Privacy Policy for Edumania

Effective Date: March 23, 2026

Welcome to Edumania. This privacy policy explains how your information is collected, used, and protected when you use the Edumania mobile application (the "Application"), developed by Raju Kumar Singh (the "Service Provider"). This service is provided on an "AS IS" basis.

Information Collection and Use

The Application collects information when you download and use it. This information may include:

  • Personal Information: When you register an account, we collect your email address, name, and profile information.
  • Payment Information: If you make purchases within the app, payment information is collected and processed securely by third-party payment gateways (e.g., Razorpay). We do not store your full card or financial details on our servers.
  • Device Information: Your device's Internet Protocol address (e.g., IP address), device type, operating system version, and similar data.
  • Usage Data: The pages of the Application that you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages, and other diagnostic data.

The Application does not gather precise information about the location of your mobile device.

The Service Provider may use the information you provided to contact you from time to time to provide you with important information, required notices, account verification emails, and related communications.

Third-Party Services

The Application utilizes third-party services that may collect information used to identify you to facilitate app functionality, authentication, and payments. Below are the links to the privacy policies of third-party service providers used by the Application:

  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase (Google)
  • Razorpay
  • YouTube (via embedded video content)

Account and Data Deletion

You can stop all collection of information by the Application easily by uninstalling it. Additionally, you have the right to request the deletion of your account and associated data. You can delete your account directly from within the Application's account settings page, which will securely purge your profile from our Firebase database and authentication systems.

Data Retention

The Service Provider will retain User Provided data for as long as you use the Application and for a reasonable time thereafter. Data associated with deleted accounts is removed in accordance with our deletion protocols.

Children's Privacy

These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. The Service Provider does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. In the case the Service Provider discovers that a child under 13 has provided personal information, the Service Provider will immediately delete this from the servers. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact the Service Provider so that necessary actions can be taken.

Security

The Service Provider is concerned about safeguarding the confidentiality of your information. We provide physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect the information we process and maintain. All sensitive data (such as passwords) is securely managed via Firebase Authentication.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time for any reason. The Service Provider will notify you of any changes by updating this page with the new Privacy Policy. You are advised to consult this Privacy Policy regularly for any changes, as continued use is deemed approval of all changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding privacy while using the Application, or have questions about our practices, please contact the Service Provider via email.
